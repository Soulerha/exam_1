Anime Japan 2023
In addition to booths from all the major animation studios, there will be 42 live events, split over three stages over the two days. Highlights include appearances by voice actors from Kimetsu no Yabai, JoJo’s Bizarre Adventure: Stone Ocean, and Attack on Titan.For 2023, to cosplay at Anime Japan you needed to purchase a “cosplay ticket” (฿255.77 *) along with your admissions ticket. You need to register and pay at the cloackroom entrance on the day. This gets you access to the changing rooms and cloakrooms.
